Next Steps for TikTok App Configuration:

  1. Download the PNG icon using the button above
  2. Upload to TikTok Developer Portal in the "App Icon" section
  3. Use these URLs in your TikTok app configuration:
    • Terms of Service: http://localhost:5174/terms
    • Privacy Policy: http://localhost:5174/privacy
    • Redirect URI: http://localhost:5174/auth/tiktok/callback
  4. Enable Sandbox Mode for testing without full review
  5. Get your Client Key and Client Secret and add them to your .env.local file
